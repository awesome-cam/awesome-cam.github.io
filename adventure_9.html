Adventure Page 9

You think to yourself that you are glad you went back to save pig. He seems like a really nice pig, not that you have met many talking pigs before.

After a few minutes of running down the hall you notice a door. The door was golden and hurt your eyes to look at because it reflected all the light.

PIG: Wait. It could be a trap!

YOU: But we need to get away from the cow guards. It could be a way out!

You decide to go inside the great golden door. You can't carry pig much longer.
You run past the door and continue down the hall.
