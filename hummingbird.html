My Hummingbird Set in action: operating my little sister's ocean diorama project

My sister made this diorama for school. She let me make it come alive with motion sensors, lights and gears. I used Scratch to hook it up to her ipad so she could turn it off and on. My sister and I often use the humming bird set together. She builds things and I program them and make them move.
