Adventure Page 8

You decide to trust pigs instincs keep on running down the hall. As you are running down the hall you notice another door.

YOU: What pig! I see another door! As you slow down to take a closer look you notice it looks like the door from your cell.

Before you have time to think you see more cow guards.

PIG: Oh no! Quick inside the door he yells.

Inside you see a chair and a belt of grenades. Above the chair in the cell is an air vent.

PIG: Those grenades look dangerous. I don't think we should use them.

You decide to try climbing up the air vent instead of using the grenades.
The guards are too close. You decide to use the grenades in the hallway as a distraction to keep the guards busy.
