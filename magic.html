My Hummingbird Set in action

I first used a Hummingbird set during one of my Surrey Challenge programs last year. I enjoyed being able to program things that move! My sister made this diorama for school and she let me make it come alive with motion sensors, lights and gears. I used the Hummingbird kit components and Scratch to hook it up to her ipad so she could turn it off and on.
