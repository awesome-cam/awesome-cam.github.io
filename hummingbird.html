My Hummingbird Set in action: operating my little sister's ocean diorama project

I first used a Hummingbird set during one of my Surrey Challenge programs last year. I saved up my allowance and bought my own kit. My sister made this diorama for school and she let me make it come alive with motion sensors, lights and gears. I used the Hummingbird kit components and Scratch to hook it up to her ipad so she could turn it off and on. My sister and I often use the hummingbird set together. She builds things and I program them and make them move.
