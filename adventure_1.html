Adventure Page 1

PIG: Thank you for trusting me. I have a plan.

PIG: My plan is to use this stick of dynamite to blow a hole in the wall. Then we can escape together.

As the pig is talking you find a key under your matress. Pig should see this you think.

YOU: Pig look what I found. It's a key. It might open the door.

PIG: I don't think this is the right key

YOU: Hmmmmmm. What if pig is right and the key is not the right key. Then you will be traped in here forever. But if it's the right key you can escape.

You go with your plan to use the key to escape.
You blow up the wall with pig's dynamite.
