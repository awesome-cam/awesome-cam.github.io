The Jail Adventure Book - By Cameron (written 2017)

You wake up at 6am when your alarm goes off. You think you are waking up in your bedroom but.... You find yourself in a jail instead.

You look around. There is nothing in the cell except a toilet and a sink. The door is locked. All of a sudden. The door opens and in comes a guard with a name tage that says "Mr Sir."

MR. SIR: Backaway from the door meatbag. I have a new cellmate for you. Hopefully you two meatbags will get along. Ha, ha, ha.

He steps out and locks the door. Your new cellmate is a pig. The pig turns to you and introduces himself.

PIG: I am pig. Nice to meet you. Do you want to escape with me?

You choose to trust the pig.
You say no. This pig looks shifty.
